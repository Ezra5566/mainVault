# Product Validation Framework

> Systematic approach to **prove market desirability** before full build.

## Phases
1. **Discovery** – 15+ user interviews, pain‑point mapping.  
2. **Concept Test** – low‑fidelity mockup + survey (n ≥ 100).  
3. **Landing‑Page Test** – run ads, capture email interest (CTR ≥ 5%).  
4. **Pre‑Sale Offer** – early‑bird pricing, collect payment commitments.  
5. **Technical Feasibility Spike** – build a *spike* prototype (max 2 weeks).  
6. **Decision Gate** – go/no‑go based on predefined success criteria.

## Success Criteria (example)
- **Interest Score** ≥ 4/5 (survey).  
- **Conversion Rate** ≥ 3% from landing‑page to email sign‑up.  
- **Pre‑Sale Revenue** ≥ KSh 150,000 in first week.  
- **Technical Spike** meets performance target (e.g., 90% accuracy).
