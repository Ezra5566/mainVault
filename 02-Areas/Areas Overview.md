# Areas of Responsibility

## Definition
Areas are ongoing spheres of activity that require maintenance and attention over time. Unlike projects, areas don't have a definitive end date.

## Key Areas

### Health & Wellness
- Physical fitness
- Mental health
- Nutrition
- Sleep hygiene
- Preventive care

### Finance & Wealth
- Budgeting & expenses
- Savings & investments
- Debt management
- Retirement planning
- Insurance

### Relationships
- Family
- Friends & social
- Romantic partner
- Professional network
- Community involvement

### Personal Growth
- Learning & education
- Skill development
- Hobbies & interests
- Spirituality
- Creativity

### Home & Environment
- Living space organization
- Home maintenance
- Digital decluttering
- Sustainable practices
- Safety & security

### Professional Development
- Career advancement
- Skill building
- Networking
- Personal branding
- Work-life balance

## Maintenance Practices
### Weekly Review
- Review each area's status
- Identify needed actions
- Update metrics or KPIs
- Celebrate progress

### Quarterly Assessment
- Deep dive into each area
- Set intentions for next quarter
- Adjust systems and routines
- Realign with values and goals

## Tracking & Metrics
Consider tracking leading indicators for each area:
- Health: Workouts per week, sleep hours
- Finance: Savings rate, expense categories
- Relationships: Quality time hours, check-ins
- Growth: Books read, courses completed
- Home: Maintenance tasks completed
- Professional: Skills learned, connections made
