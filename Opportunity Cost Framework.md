# Opportunity Cost Framework

> **Decision‑making tool** that quantifies the hidden cost of choosing one path over another.

## Scoring Formula
```
Opportunity Cost Score = (Expected Value × Probability) ÷ Time Investment
```
- **Expected Value** – projected net profit (KSh).  
- **Probability** – confidence (0‑1).  
- **Time Investment** – months required (higher denominator reduces score).

## Usage Steps
1. List all viable initiatives.  
2. Fill in **EV**, **P**, **TI** for each.  
3. Compute score → rank.  
4. Allocate resources to top‑scoring initiatives first.

## Example (2026 Q3)
| Initiative | EV (KSh) | P | TI (mo) | Score |
|------------|----------|---|----------|-------|
| Launch AI SaaS | 500,000 | 0.4 | 6 | 3,333 |
| Freelance project X | 150,000 | 0.8 | 2 | 60,000 |
| Real‑estate down‑payment | 300,000 | 0.6 | 12 | 1,500 |
