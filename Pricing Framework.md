# Pricing Framework

> **Value‑based** pricing model for AI services and products.

## Pricing Tiers
| Tier | Target Client | Service | Price (KSh) | Delivery |
|------|---------------|---------|------------|----------|
| **Starter** | Solo entrepreneurs | 1‑module AI script | 30,000 | 1 week |
| **Growth** | SMEs | End‑to‑end automation (API + UI) | 150,000 | 4 weeks |
| **Enterprise** | Large firms | Custom AI platform + support | 500,000+ | Ongoing |

## Pricing Methodologies
- **Hourly Rate:** KSh 5,000‑10,000 (based on expertise).  
- **Project‑Based:** Fixed price after scoping.  
- **Value‑Based:** % of generated revenue (e.g., 5% of saved costs).  

## Discount Policy
- **Volume Discount:** 10% for contracts ≥ 3 months.  
- **Student Discount:** 5% for verified university projects.
