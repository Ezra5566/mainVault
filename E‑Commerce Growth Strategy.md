# E‑Commerce Growth Strategy

> **Scale** an online marketplace for Kenyan SMEs selling Chinese imports.

## Growth Levers
| Lever | Tactic | KPI | Timeline |
|------|--------|-----|----------|
| **SEO** | Optimize product pages for “imported electronics Kenya” | Organic traffic ↑ 30% | Q1‑Q2 |
| **Referral** | Offer 5% commission to existing buyers for referrals | Referral rate ↑ 10% | Ongoing |
| **Paid Ads** | Targeted Facebook/Google campaigns (CPC) | ROAS > 3× | Continuous |
| **Content** | Blog series on “How to choose reliable Chinese suppliers” | Leads generated ↑ 20% | Monthly |
| **Bundling** | Create bundle deals (e.g., phone + charger) | Avg. order value ↑ 15% | Q2 |

## Customer Retention
- **Loyalty Program** – points per purchase, redeemable for discounts.  
- **Email Drip** – post‑purchase onboarding, upsell suggestions.  
- **Customer Support** – 24‑h chat, FAQ knowledge base (Obsidian).  

## next actions
- Set up **Google Analytics** and **Search Console** for the store.  
- Draft **3 SEO‑optimized blog posts** (keywords: “import phones Kenya”).  
- Design **referral reward structure** and integrate into checkout flow.
