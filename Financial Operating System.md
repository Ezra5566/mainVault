# Financial Operating System (FinOS)

> A **repeatable, auditable** set of processes that keep my money flowing and growing.

## Core Processes
1. **Budget Monitoring** – weekly review of expenses vs. budget.  
2. **Cash‑Flow Forecasting** – 3‑month forward projection using rolling averages.  
3. **Expense Approval Workflow** – any spend > KSh 5,000 requires a 2‑hour justification.  
4. **Investment Gate** – every new investment must pass **IRR > 15%** and **alignment with thesis**.  
5. **Performance Dashboard** – monthly KPI: net‑worth, cash‑flow, ROI.

## Tools
- **Obsidian Finance Vault** (this note, linked notes).  
- **Google Sheets** for real‑time tracking.  
- **Zapier** to auto‑log bank transactions.
