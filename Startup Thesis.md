# Startup Thesis

> **Why a new venture** will succeed and create lasting value.

## Core Belief
- **AI‑enabled automation** will become the default operating system for SMEs in East Africa within the next 5 years.  
- **Cost‑effective import pipelines** from China will empower these SMEs to access globally competitive products.  

## Value Proposition
Provide **plug‑and‑play AI tools** that optimize inventory, pricing, and customer‑service for Kenyan retailers importing from China.

## Business Model
- **SaaS subscription** (tiered $15‑$150/month).  
- **Revenue share** on imported goods (optional).  
- **Marketplace fees** from a curated product catalog.  

## next actions
- Draft a **1‑page pitch deck** summarising thesis, market, product, go‑to‑market.  
- Identify **3 early‑adopter retailers** for pilot testing.  
- Secure **seed funding** (KSh 5 M) from friends/family by Q1 2027.
