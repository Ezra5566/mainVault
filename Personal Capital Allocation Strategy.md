# Personal Capital Allocation Strategy

> **Purpose:** Deploy capital in a way that maximises long‑term growth while preserving liquidity.

## Allocation Model (2026‑2031)

| Asset Class | Target % | Rationale |
|-------------|----------|-----------|
| **Equity – AI Start‑ups** | 35% | High upside, synergistic with skill set |
| **Equity – Kenyan SMEs** | 20% | Market entry, currency diversification |
| **Real Estate (Kenya)** | 15% | Tangible asset, rental cash flow |
| **Index Funds (Global)** | 20% | Low‑cost diversification, passive growth |
| **Cash / Working Capital** | 10% | Buffer for pivots & opportunities |

## Rebalancing Rules
- **Quarterly** review of asset performance.  
- **Rebalance** when any class deviates > 10% from target.  
- **Reinvest** dividends & profits immediately into under‑weighted assets.
