# Product Research Framework

> Structured approach to **validate market‑fit** before building.

## 5‑Step Process
1. **Problem Discovery** – ethnographic interviews (10 + Kenyan SMEs).  
2. **Solution Sketch** – low‑fidelity mockups, value proposition canvas.  
3. **Market Sizing** – TAM/SAM/SOM calculations (using UN data).  
4. **Competitive Mapping** – direct vs. indirect, SWOT.  
5. **Validation Test** – MVP landing page + pre‑order button; measure CTR > 5%.

## Tools
- **Google Trends** for demand signals.  
- **SurveyMonkey** for structured questionnaires.  
- **Obsidian** for note‑taking and link‑graph of insights.
