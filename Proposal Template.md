# Proposal Template

> **Standardized** proposal used for every client engagement.

## Sections
1. **Cover Page** – title, client name, date.  
2. **Executive Summary** – 1‑paragraph problem & solution.  
3. **Scope of Work** – bullet list of deliverables, milestones, timeline.  
4. **Pricing** – tier, payment schedule, late‑fee policy.  
5. **Assumptions & Dependencies** – data access, client resources.  
6. **Success Metrics** – KPIs to be achieved.  
7. **Next Steps** – contract signing, kickoff meeting.

## Formatting Tips
- Use **company branding** (logo, colors).  
- Keep **PDF** under 5 pages.  
- Insert **version number** for tracking.
