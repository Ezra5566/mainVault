# AI Major — Complete Structured Summary

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the discipline of designing systems that can **perceive, learn, reason, communicate, and make decisions** with varying levels of autonomy.

Core capabilities:

* Learning from data
* Pattern recognition
* Decision making
* Language understanding
* Computer perception
* Prediction and generation
* Human–computer interaction
