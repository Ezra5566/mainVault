# Risk Assessment Framework

> Identify, evaluate, and mitigate risks for strategic initiatives.

## Risk Taxonomy
| Category | Example | Likelihood (1‑5) | Impact (1‑5) | Score (L × I) | Mitigation |
|----------|---------|------------------|--------------|---------------|------------|
| **Market** | Demand lower than expected | 3 | 4 | 12 | Conduct early validation, pivot quickly |
| **Regulatory** | Import tariff changes | 2 | 5 | 10 | Keep legal counsel, maintain flexibility |
| **Operational** | Supplier delays | 4 | 3 | 12 | Diversify suppliers, hold safety stock |
| **Financial** | Currency fluctuation | 3 | 4 | 12 | Hedge FX, maintain USD reserve |
| **Technical** | Model performance fails | 2 | 5 | 10 | Conduct regular testing, fallback model |

## Scoring Guide
- **Score ≥ 15** → **High priority** – immediate mitigation plan.  
- **Score 8‑14** → **Medium** – monitor quarterly.  
- **Score < 8** → **Low** – periodic review.
