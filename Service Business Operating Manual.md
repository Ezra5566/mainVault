# Service Business Operating Manual

> **Standard Operating Procedure** for delivering AI‑focused freelance services.

## Process Flow
1. **Lead Capture** – inbound via LinkedIn, referrals, Upwork.  
2. **Qualification Call** – 30‑min discovery, budget alignment.  
3. **Proposal** – scoped SOW, timeline, pricing (see Pricing Framework).  
4. **Contract** – signed via DocuSign, 50% upfront.  
5. **Execution** – sprint‑based delivery (2‑week sprints).  
6. **QA & Review** – client walkthrough, bug‑fixes.  
7. **Invoice & Close** – final 50% payment, request testimonial.

## Tools Stack
- **Project Management:** ClickUp (custom workflow).  
- **Version Control:** GitHub + CI (GitHub Actions).  
- **Time Tracking:** Toggl (billable hours).  
- **Communication:** Slack (client channel) + Email.

## KPI Dashboard
- **Billable Hours / month**  
- **Utilization Rate** (billable ÷ total)  
- **Client Satisfaction (NPS)**  
- **Revenue per Project**
