# Personal Decision Framework

> **Systematic** way to choose among multiple life‑ or business‑options.

## Decision Matrix
| Option | Cost (KSh) | Time (months) | Impact (1‑5) | Risk (1‑5) | Weighted Score |
|--------|------------|---------------|--------------|-----------|----------------|
| Option A | 150,000 | 3 | 4 | 2 |  (150k/1000)*0.2 + 4*0.5 – 2*0.3 = **1.2** |
| Option B | 80,000 | 2 | 3 | 1 | **0.9** |
| Option C | 250,000 | 6 | 5 | 4 | **0.7** |

*Weights: Cost 0.2, Time 0.1, Impact 0.4, Risk 0.3.

## Process
1. **List alternatives**.  
2. **Score each** on cost, time, impact, risk.  
3. **Apply weights** (adjust per context).  
4. **Rank** by total weighted score.  
5. **Select** top‑scoring option; document rationale.

## next actions

- Capture the scores in an Obsidian note for future reference.  
- Review quarterly to calibrate weights.
