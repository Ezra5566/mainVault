# Import Pipeline

> End‑to‑end workflow from **order placement** to **Kenyan market entry**.

## Stages
1. **Supplier Selection** – using Supplier Evaluation Framework.  
2. **Purchase Order (PO)** – negotiate terms, incoterms (FOB, CIF).  
3. **Shipping & Customs** – arrange freight, pay duties, obtain clearance.  
4. **Last‑Mile Delivery** – inland transport to Nairobi warehouse.  
5. **Inventory Management** – receive, inspect, store, list on e‑commerce.  
6. **After‑Sales** – warranty, returns, customer support.

## Key Documents
- **Commercial Invoice**  
- **Bill of Lading**  
- **Certificate of Origin**  
- **Import Declaration Form**  

## Compliance Checklist
- **Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS)** certification (if required).  
- **Anti‑counterption** verification.  
- **Restricted Items** check (e.g., electronics classification).
