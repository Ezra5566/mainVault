# Knowledge Compounding Framework

> **System** to ensure that learning compounds into higher‑order value over time.

## Core Principle
- **Capture** – store every insight, article, or experiment in Obsidian.  
- **Connect** – link new notes to existing ones (use wiki links).  
- **Review** – weekly/quarterly review of related notes.  
- **Re‑apply** – turn insights into actions (e.g., product tweaks, process changes).  

## Operating Steps
1. **Daily Capture** – 5‑minute note after reading or completing a task.  
2. **Weekly Link‑Audit** – run a search for “orphaned” notes, connect them.  
3. **Monthly Deep‑Dive** – pick a theme (e.g., “AI logistics”) and synthesize a summary note.  
4. **Quarterly Teaching** – write a blog post or record a video on a synthesized concept.  

## Metrics
- **Notes Created** (count).  
- **Backlinks per note** (average).  
- **Ideas Implemented** (count).
