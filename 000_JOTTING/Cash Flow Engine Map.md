# Cash Flow Engine Map

> Visual diagram of **how money moves** through my personal economy.

## Engine Components
1. **Service Delivery** – freelance AI development → immediate cash inflow.  
2. **Productization** – convert high‑margin services into SaaS/subscriptions → recurring revenue.  
3. **Capital Recycling** – funnel profits into investments (equity, real‑estate).  
4. **Automation Layer** – scripts, bots, and APIs that reduce manual effort → higher margin.

## Flow Diagram (textual)
```
Service → Revenue → Reinvest → Asset Growth → Cash Flow (repeat)
```
