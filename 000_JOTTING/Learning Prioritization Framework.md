# Learning Prioritization Framework

> Choose **what to study** to maximize impact on wealth & AI goals.

## Prioritization Matrix
| Criteria | Weight | Explanation |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| **Relevance to Goal** | 0.4 | Directly advances wealth or AI skill. |
| **Leverage** | 0.3 | Ability to multiply output (e.g., teach others). |
| **Interest** | 0.15 | Sustains motivation. |
| **Accessibility** | 0.15 | Resources available now. |

## Scoring Process
1. Rate each criterion 1‑5.  
2. Multiply by weight, sum → **Priority Score**.  
2. Sort descending; pick top‑3 for the next month.

## Example
| Course | Relevance | Leverage | Interest | Accessibility | Score |
|--------|-----------|----------|----------|----------------|-------|
| Deep Learning Specialization | 5 | 4 | 4 | 3 | **4.25** |
| JavaScript Bootcamp | 2 | 1 | 5 | 5 | **1.95** |
| Advanced Taxation (Kenya) | 3 | 2 | 3 | 4 | **2.55** |

## next actions
