# China Advantage Thesis

> **Why China** is the optimal launchpad for a Kenya‑focused import business.

## Core Advantages
| Advantage | Evidence |
|-----------|----------|
| **Manufacturing Cost** | 30‑40% lower unit cost vs. Kenya/US suppliers. |
| **Supply‑Chain Maturity** | 2‑year lead times for electronics, textiles. |
| **Logistics Hub** | Port of Shanghai + Nairobi corridor (Lamu) = 14‑day sea freight. |
| **Regulatory Incentives** | Free‑trade zones, tax rebates for export‑oriented firms. |
| **Talent Pool** | Large engineering talent for OEM partnerships. |

## Risks & Mitigations
- **Cultural/Language Barrier** – hire bilingual liaison, use local agents.  
- **Currency Volatility** – hedge via forward contracts, maintain USD reserve.  
- **Quality Control** – third‑party inspection, sample‑first policy.

- Draft **MOQ & pricing** sheets for each.  
- Set up a **video conference** with a Kenya trade office for import permits.
