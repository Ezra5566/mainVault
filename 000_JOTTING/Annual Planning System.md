# Annual Planning System

> **Year‑long roadmap** that aligns personal, financial, and business goals.

## Process
1. **Reflect** – review past year’s KPIs, wins, failures.  
2. **Set Objectives** – 3‑5 high‑level objectives (e.g., “Reach KSh 1.2 M net worth”).  
3. **Breakdown** – create quarterly objectives that feed the annual vision.  
4. **Key Results** – define measurable KR for each objective (OKR style).  
5. **Review Cadence** – monthly check‑ins, quarterly deep‑review, annual retro.

## Template Sections
- **Vision Statement**  
- **Quarterly Goals** (list)  
- **Monthly Focus** (single priority)  
- **Risk Register** (linked to [[Risk Assessment Framework]])  
- **Resource Allocation** (budget, time, people)
