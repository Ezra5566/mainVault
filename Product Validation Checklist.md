# Product Validation Checklist

> **Pre‑launch** gate to ensure a product meets market & operational criteria.

## Checklist Items
- [ ] **Problem‑Solution Fit** – validated through at least 5 user interviews.  
- [ ] **Market Size** – TAM > $1M (USD) or equivalent KSh.  
- [ ] **Competitive Gap** – clear differentiation from existing solutions.  
- [ ] **Technical Feasibility** – MVP can be built within 8 weeks.  
- [ ] **Revenue Model** – clear pricing & payment flow defined.  
- [ ] **Regulatory Compliance** – no pending legal blockers.  
- [ ] **Cost Structure** – unit economics positive at 50% capacity.  
- [ ] **Go‑to‑Market Plan** – at least 3 acquisition channels identified.  
- [ ] **Prototype Demo** – functional demo with real data.  
- [ ] **User Feedback** – ≥ 70% of testers express willingness to pay.  

## Scoring
- Assign **1‑5** to each item; total ≥ 35 / 50 triggers **Go‑to‑Market**.
