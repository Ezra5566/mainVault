## HSK 2
21. 我昨天去图书馆。 (wǒ zuótiān qù túshūguǎn) – I went to the library yesterday.
22. 他今天吃了什么？ (tā jīntiān chīle shénme?) – What did he eat today?
23. 我们下午要去购物。 (wǒmen xiàwǔ yào qù gòuwù) – We are going shopping in the afternoon.
24. 这件衣服很贵。 (zhè jiàn yīfu hěn guì) – This piece of clothing is expensive.
25. 我喜欢春天的花。 (wǒ xǐhuān chūntiān de huā) – I like the flowers of spring.
26. 他的名字叫李华。 (tā de míngzì jiào Lǐ Huá) – His name is Li Hua.
27. 我们将要旅行。 (wǒmen jiāngyào lǚxíng) – We are going to travel.
28. 她的声音很甜。 (tā de shēngyīn hěn tián) – Her voice is sweet.
29. 这道菜很辣。 (zhè dào cài hěn là) – This dish is very spicy.
30. 我需要帮助。 (wǒ xūyào bāngzhù) – I need help.
31. 他每周去健身房。 (tā měizhōu qù jiànshēnfáng) – He goes to the gym every week.
32. 我们的计划很完美。 (wǒmen de jìhuà hěn wánměi) – Our plan is perfect.
33. 这件事很重要。 (zhè jiàn shì hěn zhòngyào) – This matter is very important.
34. 我喜欢阅读小说。 (wǒ xǐhuān yuèdú xiǎoshuō) – I like reading novels.
35. 他们的房子很大。 (tāmen de fángzi hěn dà) – Their house is very big.

37. 你可以帮我吗？ (nǐ kěyǐ bāng wǒ ma?) – Can you help me?
38. 这件事我已经知道了。 (zhè jiàn shì wǒ yǐjīng zhī Dao le) – I already know about this.
39. 她喜欢听音乐。 (tā xǐhuān tīng yīnyuè) – She likes listening to music.
40. 我们准备去旅行。 (wǒmen zhǔnbèi qù lǚxíng) – We are preparing to travel.

## HSK 4
61. 我希望明天可以早点起床。 (wǒ xīwàng míngtiān kěyǐ zǎodiǎn qǐchuáng) – I hope I can get up early tomorrow.
62. 他继承自一个古老的家族。 (tā jìchéng zì yī gè gǔlǎo de jiāzōng) – He inherits from an ancient family.
63. 这道菜的主要成分是鱼和虾。 (zhè dào cài de zhǔyào chéngfèn shì yú hé xiā) – The main ingredients of this dish are fish and shrimp.
64. 我在这个问题上遇到了一些困难。 (wǒ zài zhège wèntí shàng yùdào le yīxiē kùnnán) – I encountered some difficulties in this issue.
65. 我们将在下个月开始新的项目。 (wǒmen jiāng zài xià yuè gēn qǐ xīn de xiàngmù) – We will start a new project next month.
66. 他经常去图书馆学习。 (tā jīngcháng qù túshūguǎn xuéxí) – He often studies at the library.
67. 这是一个重要的发现。 (zhè shì yī gè zhòngyào de fāxiàn) – This is an important discovery.
68. 他对这个话题非常感兴趣。 (tā duì zhège huàtí fēicháng gǎn xìngqù) – He is very interested in this topic.
69. 我昨晚没有睡够。 (wǒ zuówǎn méiyǒu shuì gòu) – I didn't sleep enough last night.
70. 这件事是否可以再检查一次？ (zhè jiàn shì shìfǒu kěyǐ zài jiǎnchá yīcì?) – Can this matter be checked again?
71. 他喜欢在海边散步。 (tā xǐhuān zài hǎibiān sànbù) – He likes to take walks by the sea.
72. 我的目标是成为一名医生。 (wǒ de mùbiāo shì chéngwéi yī míng yīshēng) – My goal is to become a doctor.
73. 我们需要准备更多的材料。 (wǒmen xūyào zhǔnbèi gèng duō de cáiliào) – We need to prepare more materials.
74. 这部电影很感人。 (zhè bù diànyǐng hěn gǎn rén) – This movie is moving.
75. 我对他的选择表示赞同。 (wǒ duì tā de xuǎnzé biǎoshì zàn tóng) – I approve of his choice.
76. 他正在写一篇论文。 (tā zhèngzài xiě yī piān lùnwén) – He is writing a paper.
77. 这道题很难解决。 (zhè dào tí hěn nán jiějué) – This problem is hard to solve.
78. 我们应该更加关注环境保护。 (wǒmen yīnggāi gèngjiā guānzhù huánjìng bǎohù) – We should pay more attention to environmental protection.
79. 他经常帮助朋友。 (tā jīngcháng bāngzhù péngyǒu) – He often helps his friends.
80. 我期待明天的聚会。 (wǒ qīdài míngtiān de jùhuì) – I look forward to tomorrow's gathering.

## HSK 5
81. 在他的影响下，我开始思考哲学问题。 (zài tā de yǐngxiāo xià, wǒ kāishǐ sīgāo zhéxué wèntí) – Under his influence, I began to think about philosophical questions.
82. 我们需要制定一个长期的计划。 (wǒmen xūyào zhìgěi yī gè chángqī de jìhuà) – We need to devise a long-term plan.
83. 这部作品展示了不同文化之间的相互影响。 (zhè bù zuòpǐn zhǎnshìle bùtóng wénhuà zhījiān de xiānghù yǐngxiǎng) – This work showcases the mutual influence between different cultures.
84. 他提出了许多关于社会公平的建议。 (tā tí chūle yǔn zhōng duō guānyú shèhuì gōngpíng de jiànyì) – He put forward many suggestions regarding social fairness.
85. 我无法完全理解他的动机。 (wǒ wúfāng quán lǐjiě tā de dòngjī) – I cannot fully understand his motives.
86. 他们正在为下一届奥运会做准备。 (tāmen zhèngzài wèi xià yī gé Àoyùnhuì zuò zhǔnbèi) – They are preparing for the next Olympic Games.
87. 这件事的关键在于如何平衡利益。 (zhè jiàn shì de guāndī yú rúhé pínghéng lìyì) – The key to this matter lies in how to balance interests.
88. 我喜欢阅读经典文学。 (wǒ xǐhuān yuèdú jīngdiǎn wénxué) – I like reading classic literature.
89. 我们在讨论一个很复杂的问题。 (wǒmen zài tǎolùn yī gè hěn fùzá de wèntí) – We are discussing a very complex issue.
90. 他最近在学习一个新的语言。 (tā zuìjìn zài xuéxí yī gè xīn de yǔyán) – He has recently been learning a new language.
91. 这部电影的导演很有名。 (zhè bù diànyǐng de dǎoyǎn hěn yǒu míng) – The director of this movie is famous.
92. 我期待看到更多的进步。 (wǒ qīdài kàn dào gèng duō de jìnbù) – I look forward to seeing more progress.
93. 他的话里总是充满智慧。 (tā de huà lǐ zǒng shì chōngmǎn zhìhuì) – His words are always full of wisdom.
94. 我们将在明天之前完成所有的工作。 (wǒmen jiāng zài míngtiān zhīqián wánchéng suǒyǒu de gōngzuò) – We will finish all the work before tomorrow.
95. 这道菜的味道非常独特。 (zhè dào cài de wèidao fēicháng dútè) – The flavor of this dish is very unique.
96. 我喜欢在安静的地方阅读。 (wǒ xǐhuān zài ānjìng de dìfāng yuèdú) – I like to read in a quiet place.
97. 他们正在组织一次大型的讨论。 (tāmen zhèngzài zǔzhī yī cì dàxíng de tǎolùn) – They are organizing a large-scale discussion.
98. 我很感激他的帮助。 (wǒ hěn gǎnjì tā de bāngzhù) – I am very grateful for his help.
99. 我们需要更多的时间来完成这个项目。 (wǒmen xūyào gèng duō de shíjiān lái wánchéng zhège xiàngmù) – We need more time to complete this project.
100. 他的发明改变了整个世界。 (tā de fāmíng gǎibiàn le zhěnggè shìjiè) – His invention changed the whole world.

*Tip: Practice each sentence by reading the Chinese, then the pinyin, then the English translation. Try writing the sentence on grid paper, then say it aloud, and finally use the English meaning to create your own sentence.*
