# AI Product Builder Roadmap

> From **technical expertise** to **product‑centric entrepreneurship**.

## Phases
| Phase | Goal | Key Activities |
|-------|------|----------------|
| **Discovery** | Validate a market pain | Customer interviews, problem tree, market sizing |
| **Design** | Define product vision | Story mapping, UX wireframes, pricing hypothesis |
| **Build** | Develop MVP | Rapid prototyping, model fine‑tuning, API integration |
| **Launch** | Acquire first users | Launch plan, growth hacks, feedback loop |
| **Scale** | Optimize and expand | Automated pipelines, internationalization, partnership |

## Core Competencies
- **Problem Framing** – turning vague user pain into measurable outcomes.  
- **Rapid Experimentation** – A/B testing, smoke‑test funnels.  
- **Monetization** – subscription, usage‑based, freemium models.  
- **Data‑Driven Iteration** – analytics stack (Amplitude, Mixpanel).

## next actions
- Pick **one market segment** in Kenya (e.g., agritech, health‑tech).  
- Conduct **5 customer interviews** by end of Q1 2027.  
- Draft a **problem‑solution canvas** for that segment.
