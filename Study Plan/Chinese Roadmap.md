# English Learning Roadmap (Undergraduate Level)

## Overall Goals
- Systematically master modern Chinese phonology, vocabulary, grammar, and writing expression
- Be able to read and write various texts fluently, completing basic academic writing

## Time Planning
- Current Phase: Ongoing
- Short-term Goals (0-3 months): Basic consonants, vowels, simple sentences
- Mid-term Goals (3-12 months): Intermediate grammar, reading short texts
- Long-term Goals (1+ years): University-level reading of academic texts, writing reports

## Milestones
1. Complete consonant and vowel pronunciation practice (2 weeks)
2. Memorize and use 500 basic vocabulary words (3 months)
3. Read and translate simple news articles (3-6 months)
4. Complete short essay writing exercises (6-9 months)
5. Participate in group discussions and presentations (9-12 months)

## Recommended Resources
- Textbooks: "Chinese Phonology"
- Online Platforms: Duolingo, HelloChinese
- Reading: "Primary School Language Textbook" (People's Education Press) Lower Grades texts
- Writing: Academic Writing Guide

## Learning Methods
- Daily 30-minute pronunciation practice
- Weekly 1 conversation practice session
- Mark new vocabulary during reading, weekly review
- Submit drafts of writing weekly

## Tracking and Evaluation
- Monthly tests
- Semester-end self-assessment
- Teacher or peer feedback
