# Marketplace Analysis Kenya

> **Competitive landscape** of existing Kenyan e‑commerce & import platforms.

## Key Players
| Platform | Focus | Avg. Monthly Visitors | Pricing Model |
|----------|-------|-----------------------|---------------|
| **Jumia** | General marketplace | 2 M | Listing fee + transaction % |
| **Konga** | Electronics & home | 800 k | Fixed listing fee |
| **M‑Pesa Shop** | Mobile money commerce | 1.2 M | Low‑fee per transaction |
| **LocalBazaar** | SME handmade | 150 k | No fee for first 6 months |

## Market Gaps
- **Niche import‑focused marketplace** (Chinese tech products).  
- **Low‑cost logistics integration** for small sellers.  
- **Localized payment options** (M‑Pesa, mobile wallets).  

## SWOT Summary
- **Strength:** First‑mover in niche import segment.  
- **Weakness:** Limited brand awareness.  
- **Opportunity:** Growing middle‑class demand for affordable electronics.  
- **Threat:** Large players could launch similar niche sections.

## next actions
- Map **10 niche product categories** with high import volume.  
- Conduct **price‑sensitivity survey** (Google Forms).  
- Build a **minimum viable marketplace** on Shopify (or custom).
