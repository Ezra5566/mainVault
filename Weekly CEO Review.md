# Weekly CEO Review

> **Leadership rhythm** to align vision, metrics, and team progress.

## Review Agenda
1. **KPIs Snapshot** – net‑worth, cash‑flow, revenue, runway.  
2. **Objective Status** – check each quarterly objective (green/yellow/red).  
3. **Risk Update** – new risks, mitigation progress.  
4. **Decision Log** – decisions made this week, outcomes.  
5. **Next Steps** – tasks for the coming week, owners, due dates.  

## Template Note (Obsidian)
```
## Weekly CEO Review – {date}
- **KPIs**:  
  - Net‑worth: KSh {current}  
  - Cash‑flow: {positive/negative} {amount}  
- **Objectives**:  
  - {{Objective 1}} – {status}  
  - {{Objective 2}} – {status}  
- **Risks**:  
  - New risk: {description} – mitigation: {owner}  
- **Decisions**:  
  - Approved freelance contract X – $30k.  
- **Next Week Tasks**:  
  - {Task A} – owner A – due {date}  
  - {Task B} – owner B – due {date}  
```
